4. Is there a newsgroup?

One of the most important resources is the literate programming newsgroup, comp.programming.literate. Because of the amount of spamming and unrelated the posts, the newsgroup is now moderated. Posts to the newsgroup are now routed through litprog-mod@cs.virginia.edu. If your news reader does not post through this address, then you will be unable to post messages to the newgroup. You can read this newsgroup using your standard reader.
